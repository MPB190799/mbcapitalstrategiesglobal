Past performance is not indicative of future results. Dividend yields can be cut or eliminated. Currency fluctuations can amplify or eliminate returns for international investors.

The sectors covered on this website carry specific risks:

  • Shipping stocks are highly cyclical and can experience dramatic swings in revenue and share price based on freight rate movements, global trade volumes, and geopolitical disruptions.
  • Mining stocks are exposed to commodity price volatility, operational risks (geological, environmental, political), and the inherent uncertainty of resource estimation.
  • MLPs and pipeline investments carry regulatory risk, interest rate sensitivity, commodity volume exposure, and tax complexity. Distributions are not guaranteed.
  • BDCs invest in below-investment-grade debt of private companies, carrying credit risk, default risk, and potential NAV erosion during downturns.
  • High-yield investments in general carry above-average risk of capital loss. Attractive yields may reflect elevated risk.
