Our Story

About MB Capital Strategies

An independent research platform founded on transparency, real capital, and hard asset conviction.

The Mission

MB Capital Strategies Global was founded in June 2025 by Marco Bozem with a simple goal: provide honest, data-driven analysis on dividend-paying hard asset stocks — without the noise, hype, or hidden agendas that plague financial media.

We believe in cashflow over speculation. While most financial content focuses on price targets and growth stories, we focus on what actually lands in your brokerage account: dividends, distributions, and sustainable income.

About Marco Bozem

Marco has been an active investor since 2018, building a portfolio of over €60,000 focused exclusively on hard assets — shipping, pipelines, mining, and energy. Every position is real, every dividend is documented, and every analysis comes from first-hand experience managing actual capital.

Through YouTube and this website, Marco shares his research process, investment decisions, and portfolio updates with a growing community of income-focused investors.

What Makes Us Different

  • Real money at stake — This isn't a paper portfolio. Every position is backed by actual capital.
  • No price predictions — We don't forecast stock prices. We analyze cashflows and dividend sustainability.
  • No paid recommendations — We never tell you what to buy. We show you our research and let you decide.
  • Full transparency — Our portfolio is tracked live via Parqet. Holdings, gains, losses — everything is visible.
  • Free tools — All eight calculators are free, no registration required.

The Platform

MB Capital Strategies Global is the English-language extension of the established MB Capital Strategies platform, bringing the same research quality, tools, and transparency to an international audience. Whether you're a seasoned dividend investor or just starting to explore hard assets, you'll find actionable insights and practical tools here.
