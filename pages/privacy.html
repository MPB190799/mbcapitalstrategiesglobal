We process personal data only in accordance with the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the German Federal Data Protection Act (BDSG).

3. Data Collection on This Website

Server Log Files

Our hosting provider automatically collects and stores information in server log files that your browser transmits when you visit the site. This includes:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL (the page you visited before ours)
  • IP address of the requesting computer (anonymized)
  • Date and time of the server request

This data cannot be attributed to specific persons. We do not merge this data with other data sources. The data is stored on the basis of Article 6(1)(f) GDPR, as we have a legitimate interest in ensuring the technical error-free presentation and optimization of our website. Server log files are automatically deleted after 30 days.

Cookies

This website uses only technically necessary cookies that are required for the functioning of the site (e.g., session management, navigation preferences). These cookies do not track your behavior across websites and do not collect personal data for advertising purposes. The legal basis for technically necessary cookies is Article 6(1)(f) GDPR. You can configure your browser to reject cookies, though this may limit the functionality of the website.

Contact by Email

If you contact us by email, your message and the personal data you provide (name, email address, content of your inquiry) will be stored by us for the purpose of processing your request and in case of follow-up questions. We will not share this data without your consent. The processing of this data is based on Article 6(1)(b) GDPR if your inquiry relates to the performance of a contract or is necessary for pre-contractual measures, and otherwise on Article 6(1)(f) GDPR based on our legitimate interest in effectively processing inquiries. We will retain this data until you request its deletion, revoke your consent, or the purpose for data storage no longer applies.

4. Analytics and Third-Party Services

Web Analytics

This website does not use Google Analytics or any comparable third-party tracking tool. We do not collect behavioral data, create user profiles, or track you across sessions for advertising purposes. If we implement analytics in the future, we will update this policy and, where required, obtain your consent before activating any tracking.

Fonts

This website uses Google Fonts for consistent typography. When you visit a page, your browser loads the required font files from Google's servers. This transmits your IP address and browser information to Google. The use of Google Fonts is based on Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in a uniform presentation of the website). Google's privacy policy can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Hosting

This website is hosted by a third-party provider. Personal data collected on this website (primarily IP addresses in server logs) is stored on the host's servers. Our hosting provider processes data on our behalf and in accordance with a data processing agreement pursuant to Article 28 GDPR. The hosting provider does not use your data for its own purposes.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR) — You can request information about whether we process your personal data and, if so, what data we hold.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR) — You can request the correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR) — You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR) — You can request that we restrict the processing of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR) — You can request that data you have provided to us be transferred to you or to a third party in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR) — You can object to the processing of your personal data based on legitimate interests at any time, for reasons arising from your particular situation.
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR) — If processing is based on your consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory data protection authority if you believe that the processing of your personal data violates the GDPR.

6. Data Security

We use SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission to protect your data during transfer. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against accidental or intentional manipulation, partial or complete loss, destruction, or unauthorized access by third parties.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable legal retention periods. When the data is no longer needed and no legal retention obligations apply, it is deleted or anonymized.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The current version is always available on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
