Investment Calculators

Eight purpose-built calculators to model dividends, cashflows, and valuations across hard-asset sectors. No sign-up required.

Every calculator below is designed for the specific needs of income-focused investors in shipping, pipelines, mining, and high-yield sectors. Plug in real numbers from earnings reports, model different scenarios, and make data-driven allocation decisions.

Dividend Compound Calculator

Project the long-term growth of a dividend-paying position with reinvestment (DRIP). Input your starting capital, current yield, expected dividend growth rate, and time horizon to see how compounding transforms income over 5, 10, or 25 years.

Launch Calculator →

Shipping Cashflow Calculator

Model the operating cashflow of a tanker or bulk carrier fleet under different dayrate scenarios. Enter fleet size, OPEX per vessel, debt service, and spot vs. charter rates to estimate distributable cashflow per share.

Launch Calculator →

Pipeline DCF Valuation

Run a discounted cashflow analysis tailored to midstream MLPs and pipeline corporations. Factor in contracted revenues, take-or-pay volume commitments, distribution growth guidance, and terminal value to estimate intrinsic value per unit.

Launch Calculator →

Mining Break-Even Calculator

Determine the commodity price at which a mining operation becomes profitable. Input all-in sustaining costs (AISC), production volume, royalties, and overhead to find the break-even gold, copper, or lithium price per ounce or tonne.

Launch Calculator →

Yield-on-Cost Tracker

Track how your effective yield evolves over time as dividends grow. Enter your original cost basis and the current annualized distribution to calculate your yield-on-cost — the metric that reveals the true power of buying and holding dividend growers.

Launch Calculator →

Distribution Coverage Analyzer

Evaluate how safe a distribution is by comparing distributable cashflow to the actual payout. Input DCF per unit, distribution per unit, maintenance capex, and debt obligations to calculate the coverage ratio and assess sustainability.

Launch Calculator →

NAV Discount Calculator

Determine whether a BDC, closed-end fund, or asset-heavy company trades at a premium or discount to its net asset value. Compare market price to book value per share and track the historical NAV discount trend.

Launch Calculator →

Income Portfolio Builder

Construct a diversified income portfolio across all hard-asset sectors. Allocate capital across shipping, pipelines, mining, and BDCs, then see the projected blended yield, sector exposure breakdown, and monthly income estimate.

Launch Calculator →
