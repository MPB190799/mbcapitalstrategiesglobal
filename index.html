What is MB Capital Strategies Global?

MB Capital Strategies Global is an independent research platform for investors who focus on cashflow over price speculation. At the center are hard assets: energy, shipping, mining and midstream pipelines — sectors that convert real economic activity into reliable dividends. Dividend yields of 6–15% are not the exception here, but the result of concrete cashflow logic.

You won't find price forecasts or recommendations here. What you will find: clear analysis on dividend strategies, in-depth research on shipping & tanker stocks and mining stocks, honest cashflow valuations — and an investor who puts real capital at stake in these strategies.

The Hard Asset Strategy in a Nutshell

Hard assets generate cashflow from real economic activity. A tanker earns money when it transports oil. A pipeline collects fees when gas flows through it. A mine produces revenue when copper is extracted.

Hard asset stocks are accessible to global investors through major exchanges: tanker stocks like Frontline or Scorpio Tankers trade on NYSE, LNG carriers like FLEX LNG via international brokers, and midstream MLPs like Enbridge or ONEOK on US markets. Our Toolbox and Hard Asset Guide show you exactly how to get started.

My strategy: 80% of the portfolio in stable, cashflow-strong dividend payers. The remaining 20% stays as liquidity for counter-cyclical buys during weak phases.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are hard assets and why do they pay high dividends?
Hard assets are physical, tangible assets — ships, pipelines, mines, oil wells, infrastructure. They generate cashflow from real economic activity and distribute it as dividends. Yields of 6–15% are typical during high-cycle phases. Unlike tech stocks valued on future potential, hard asset companies are valued on what they produce today.
What is your investment philosophy?
Focus on hard assets, high yield, cashflow & counter-cyclical opportunities. 80% in stable dividend payers, 20% in tactical liquidity for weak phases. We buy assets that generate income regardless of what the broader market does.
